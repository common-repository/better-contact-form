<li>Search for 'Better Contact Form'</li>
<li>Click 'Install Now' and activate the plugin</li>

<ol>

<ol>

<li>Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in your WordPress admin area</li>
</ol>

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= My contact form is not forwarding emails to us =

If you are not receiving emails with our contact form, please check your spam box and add mailer@zotabox.com to your contact list.
 
If this does not work, try a @gmail.com recipient email address. If this works you may need to tell your local internet service provider to ‘allow’ emails from Zotabox.

= Can I add multiple contact forms to my website? =

We only have the ability to add 1 contact form to each website right now. We plan to offer this function in the next 3-4 months.

= Does my contact form integrate with Mailchimp? =

Our contact form does not integrate with Mailchimp. All correspondence is sent to your ‘recipient’ email address on the contact form setting page.
 
To collect new email subscribers, please use one of our email collection tools such as our Email Capture Popup or Email Capture Bar.
 
New subscribers from these premium tools CAN be integrated into Mailchimp.

= Does your contact form have anti-spam technology? =

Yes, our contact form uses Ajax loading/validation technology to prevent spam and robot submissions.
